# House Ashcroft

> **Campaign depth:** [[House Ashcroft - Campaign Notes]]  
> **Crest:** ![[House Ashcroft - Crest.png]]

## Members

**Headquarters:** Ashcroft Manor

**Leader(s):** Duchess Vivienne Ashcroft

**Prominent Members:**

- [[Lady Seraphine Ashcroft]]

## Ideology

Competence creates prosperity. Good intentions are meaningless without execution.

### Goals

Make systems function. Serve as ministers, treasurers, and royal advisors who keep the kingdom running.

### Means

Planning, organization, expertise.

## History

Masters of administration and governance. The Ashcrofts excel at making systems function. Where Thornfields win wars, Ashcrofts run kingdoms. For centuries they have served as ministers, treasurers, and royal advisors.

Their influence has expanded dramatically during the last century.

## Other

**Family Notability:** Politics, Commerce

**Iconography:** A silver key upon a field of deep blue.

**Relationships:** Allied with [[House Thornwood]]; competes with [[House Cogwort]]; frustrated by [[House Reed]].

**Resources:** Bureaucrats, administrators, tax networks.

Many people admire Ashcroft competence. Few find Ashcrofts warm.

# House Cogwort

> **Campaign depth:** [[House Cogwort - Campaign Notes]]  
> **Crest:** ![[House Cogwort - Crest.png]]

## Members

**Headquarters:** Cogwort Exchange Hall

**Leader(s):** Matron Grizelda Cogwort

**Prominent Members:**

- [[Brindle Cogwort]]

## Ideology

Prosperity creates opportunity. A thriving kingdom is a free kingdom.

### Goals

Earn influence through trade rather than inheritance. Build a kingdom where ordinary people can build better lives through prosperity.

### Means

Negotiation, incentives, investment.

## History

A rising mercantile house that gained power through trade rather than inheritance. The Cogworts are living proof that influence can be earned. Many old nobles resent them for it.

They have become one of the wealthiest families in Veyr within only three generations.

## Other

**Family Notability:** Commerce, Industry

**Iconography:** A brass coin balanced atop a set of scales.

**Relationships:** Competes with [[House Ashcroft]]; distrusted by [[House Thornwood]]; friendly with [[House Reed]].

**Resources:** Merchant fleets, banking interests, trade guilds.

They are often underestimated. They usually profit from it.

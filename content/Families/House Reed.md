# House Reed

> **Campaign depth:** [[House Reed - Campaign Notes]]  
> **Crest:** ![[House Reed - Crest.png]]

## Members

**Headquarters:** Reed Hall

**Leader(s):** Elder Thomas Reed

**Prominent Members:**

- [[Tobin Reed]]

## Ideology

A ruler serves the community. Leadership exists because people place their trust in it.

### Goals

Represent ordinary people and prove that common folk matter in the kingdom's future.

### Means

Consensus, trust, cooperation.

## History

Not truly noble by traditional standards. The Reeds became influential through local leadership and public trust. Their rise reflects changing attitudes throughout the kingdom. Many common folk see them as proof that ordinary people matter.

Their influence is recent but growing rapidly.

## Other

**Family Notability:** Commerce, Other (Community Leadership)

**Iconography:** A sheaf of wheat bound with red ribbon.

**Relationships:** Friendly with [[House Cogwort]]; ideological rivalry with [[House Voss]]; viewed skeptically by [[House Ashcroft]].

**Resources:** Popular support, local councils, community organizations.

They possess little traditional power. Yet everyone seems willing to listen to them.

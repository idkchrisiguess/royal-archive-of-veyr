# House Thornwood

> **Campaign depth:** [[House Thornwood - Campaign Notes]]  
> **Crest:** ![[House Thornwood - Crest.png]]

## Members

**Headquarters:** Thornwatch Keep

**Leader(s):** Lord Cedric Thornfield

**Prominent Members:**

- [[Sir Garrick Thornfield]]

## Ideology

A ruler's duty is to bear burdens others cannot. Leadership requires discipline. Power exists to protect. Duty comes before personal happiness.

### Goals

Uphold stability through service and sacrifice. Maintain the tradition of military and civic leadership established since the first generation of Crown-chosen rulers.

### Means

Honor, service, obligation, tradition.

## History

One of the oldest noble houses in Veyr. The Thornfields believe stability is earned through service and sacrifice. Many generals, governors, and monarchs have emerged from their lineage.

The Thornfields trace their ancestry to the first generation of rulers selected by the Crown. Their family history is filled with military victories and acts of public service. Many consider them the model of traditional leadership.

Their influence has slowly declined as commerce and cities have become more important than border warfare.

## Other

**Family Notability:** Military, Politics

**Iconography:** A silver sword planted point-down beneath a crown of oak leaves.

**Relationships:** Allied with [[House Ashcroft]]; respectful rivalry with [[House Voss]]; distrustful of [[House Cogwort]].

**Resources:** Veteran military officers, noble retainers, provincial governors.

Many citizens instinctively trust a Thornfield. Many nobles are tired of them.

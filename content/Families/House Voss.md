# House Voss

> **Campaign depth:** [[House Voss - Campaign Notes]]  
> **Crest:** ![[House Voss - Crest.png]]

## Members

**Headquarters:** The Lantern Abbey

**Leader(s):** High Abbess Miriam Voss

**Prominent Members:**

- [[Mother Elira Voss]]

## Ideology

Order creates peace. Freedom without structure eventually destroys itself.

### Goals

Guide rulers as custodians rather than owners of power. Provide spiritual and institutional stability during uncertain times.

### Means

Guidance, doctrine, persuasion.

## History

A powerful spiritual dynasty tied to the kingdom's major religious institutions. The Voss family claims that rulers are custodians rather than owners of power.

Their influence rises during times of instability.

## Other

**Family Notability:** Religion, Politics

**Iconography:** A golden lantern surrounded by thirteen stars.

**Relationships:** Mutual respect with [[House Thornwood]]; ideological rivalry with [[House Reed]]; concerned about [[House Cogwort]].

**Resources:** Temples, scholars, community networks.

Many trust their wisdom. Others fear their certainty.

# Choosing Traditions (22+)

Formal, political, social, emotional, humorous. None are magical evaluations unless noted as Hall *atmosphere* only.

---

## Outside the Hall

1. **The Silence** — three days after monarch's death ([[The Choosing Overview]]).
2. **The Bookmakers** — wagers on winner, first alliance, first scandal, first tears, first Hall nickname.
3. **Lantern Vigils** — thirteen lanterns; extinguish one when a candidate "falls" in public narrative (subjective).
4. **Children's Choosing** — village reenactments; folk myths about inside.
5. **Procession Wagers** — bets on who arrives last (pride vs humility reading).
6. **Blessing Lines** — commoners touch a candidate's cloak for luck (pressure).
7. **The Painted Thirteen** — tavern murals updated nightly in capital.

---

## Opening & Formal

8. **Threshold Declaration** — *"You arrive not as rulers. You arrive as possibilities."*
9. **Hand on the Crown** — anticlimax; centuries of debate.
10. **Thirteen Bells** — sealing ([[Bell Tower]]).
11. **The First Table** — first meal inside; every monarch sat here ([[The First Table Hall]]).
12. **Symbol Choosing** — each picks a door symbol in [[Thirteen Doors Corridor]].
13. **Steward's Reading** — schedule of challenges posted at dawn (tradition, not Crown script).

---

## Political & Social

14. **The Open Ledger** — candidates may publish one promise to the kingdom (magically echoed once).
15. **Pair Walk** — random partner for garden walk; alliances form or fracture.
16. **The Question Cup** — dinner game: anonymous questions in a cup; must answer or drink (water or wine, table chooses).
17. **Nickname Night** — Commons assigns Hall nicknames; sticks socially.
18. **Alliance Toast** — public toast binds reputation if broken later.
19. **The Silent Hour** — no speech in Commons; communication only by writing (tests patience).
20. **Debater's Circle** — topic drawn from library; no winners declared officially.

---

## Emotional & Reflective

21. **Lantern for the Absent** — inner vigil for those you left outside.
22. **Letter That Cannot Be Sent** — written, burned in hearth; Hall warmth responds (comfort).
23. **The Mirror Hour** — voluntary visit to [[Mirror Walk]] tradition night.
24. **Forgiveness Bench** — garden bench; two who sit must speak one true thing.

---

## Humorous & Human

25. **Fool's Permission** — one evening [[Jester Corvin]]-style license: honest jokes without punishment (once).
26. **Kitchen King** — whoever feeds the Hall chooses music that night.
27. **The Upside-Down Meal** — dessert first; reminds nobles and commons they are still people.

---

## Challenge-Adjacent (Tradition + Test blur)

28. **The Standing Oath** — repeat oath before witnesses; breaking damages Trust track heavily.

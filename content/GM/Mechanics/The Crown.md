# The Crown

---

## What It Is

Ancient magical artifact created to identify suitable rulers. After five centuries of monarchs, it no longer believes perfect rulers exist. It seeks **understanding** — who can grow into responsibility.

- **Not evil** · **Not rigged** · **Not a secret mastermind**
- A **winner will be selected**

---

## Historical Observations (In-World)

Scholars note:

- Brilliant, terrible, beloved, and unpopular rulers alike.
- Many seem **transformed** by the Hall — more thoughtful, humble, mature.
- The Crown may value change over pedigree.

---

## Philosophy (DM)

The Crown cares about:

- Growth and self-awareness
- Empathy under pressure
- Responsibility when no one applauds
- Capacity to **change** when wrong

It does **not** publish a scoreboard. Candidates may feel watched; they cannot game a known formula.

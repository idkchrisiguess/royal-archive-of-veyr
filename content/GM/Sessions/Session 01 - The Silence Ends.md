# Session 01 — The Silence Ends

**Act I** · Levels 3 · ~4 hours

---

## Summary

The Silence ends. Names appear. PCs travel, witness Threshold Ceremony, enter sealed Hall, share **[[The First Table]]**. First alliances and rivalries spark.

---

## Objectives

- Establish tone: worthiness, not apocalypse
- Introduce all seven NPC candidates + PCs
- Run Threshold + sealing + First Table
- Seed 2 relationship hooks and 1 reputation beat

---

## Scene-by-Scene

1. **Dawn of names** — Crown band; family reactions ([[Political Complication Table]] optional)
2. **Procession** — crowds, bookmakers, vigils
3. **Grand Atrium** — steward's declaration; hand on Crown
4. **Sealing** — thirteen bells; doors close
5. **First Table** — [[First Night at the Hall]]

## Big Brother Beat (Subtle)

## DM Guidance

- **Do not** explain Crown algorithm
- Hall warm, not spooky
- End on unanswered question: *"Why were we chosen?"*

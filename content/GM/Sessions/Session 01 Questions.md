# Emergency Questions

Keep these nearby when conversation dies.

Any NPC can ask them.

### Light

> "What is one thing nobody at this table knows about you?"

> "What did you expect the Hall to be like?"

> "Who here surprised you today?"

---

### Personal

> "What do you hope happens during the Choosing?"

> "What do you fear might happen?"

> "What would make you leave willingly?"

---

### Dangerous

> "Who at this table do you trust least?"

> "Who here seems most prepared?"

> "Do you think the Crown can make mistakes?"

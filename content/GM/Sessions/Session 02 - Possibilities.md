# Session 02 — Possibilities

**Act I** · Level 3 · ~4 hours

---

## Summary

First full day inside the sealed Hall: daily rhythm, **Symbol Choosing**, **Pair Walk**, social games. Thirteen candidates (seven established + six new faces) negotiate space and story.

---

## Objectives

- Establish [[The Commons]] as home base
- Run Symbol Choosing + Pair Walk ([[Choosing Traditions]])
- Give each of **13 NPC pillars** a spotlight beat (including [[Dame Lysa Fairward]], [[Aldric Penn]], [[Captain Mira Windale]], [[Archivist Thalen Moss]], [[Sister Kesh Oaken]], [[Healer Rowan Sable]])
- Seed 2 alliances and 1 rivalry on [[Alliance Tracker]]

---

## Scene-by-Scene

1. **Morning bulletin** — [[The Commons]]: challenge schedule appears; bookmakers' odds echoed via servant gossip ([[Candidate Rumor Table]] optional).
2. **Symbol Choosing** — [[Thirteen Doors Corridor]]: each candidate marks a door; explain symbol publicly or refuse (−1 Standing, recoverable).
3. **Pair Walk** — [[Gardens of Reflection]]: steward assigns pairs (roll or deliberate); 20–30 min RP walk; Hall may shorten paths ([[Hall Event Generator]]).
4. **Debate preview** — [[Debate Hall]]: speaking stone tests *self*-honesty only; foreshadow Session 03.
5. **Labyrinth icebreaker** — [[The Labyrinth Lounge]]: group must laugh together to leave; evening tone.

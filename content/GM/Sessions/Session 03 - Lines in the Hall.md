# Session 03 — Lines in the Hall

**Act I** · Level 3 · ~4 hours

---

## Summary

Act I climax: pressure from outside ([[Political Complication Table]]), **Debater's Circle**, [[Whisper Gallery]] mishap, public vs private alliances. Lines drawn before Act II challenges.

---

## Objectives

- Deliver family/house pressure via letters
- Run Debater's Circle ([[Debate Hall]])
- Consequence from overheard secret
- End Act I with visible alliance or rivalry

---

## Scene-by-Scene

1. **Letters** — [[Steward's Nook]]: one [[Political Complication Table]] per relevant PC/NPC; read aloud or hide.
2. **Debater's Circle** — [[Debate Hall]]: draw topic (tax coast/heartlands, abbey law, succession reform); speaking stone stumbles on self-lies.
3. **Whisper mishap** — [[Whisper Gallery]]: kind or cruel secret to wrong listener ([[Choosing Challenges#3]] seed).
4. **Forgiveness Bench** (optional) — [[Gardens of Reflection]]: two candidates sit; one refuses → relationship clock.
5. **Night rumor** — [[The Commons]]: roll [[Candidate Rumor Table]]; affects Public Perception.

# Session 04 — The First Challenge

**Act II** · Level 4 · ~4 hours

---

## Summary

Act II opens with **[[Choosing Challenges#1 The Divided Harvest]]** or **#12 The Baker's Feast** — scarcity, class, cooperation. Reputation swings matter.

---

## Objectives

- Run first major challenge (food/resource)
- Teach challenge schedule rhythm
- Tie outcome to [[Reputation System]] without "winning the Crown"

---

## Scene-by-Scene

1. **Briefing** — [[Steward's Nook]]: rules; no elimination — consequences are social.
2. **Challenge** — [[Kitchens and Hearth]] / [[The Commons]]: allocate scarce supplies or cook/serve together.
3. **Blame or credit** — [[Debate Hall]] or Commons: who hoarded? who shared?
4. **Recovery** — [[The Hearth Below]] optional: allies debrief; Hall fire pops on self-deception.

## Optional Encounters

- [[Hall Event Generator]]: luxury item runs out
- Sabotage *rumor* — social investigation, not poison

---

## Relationship Opportunities

- Alliance to "game" scarcity fairly
- PC sacrifices share → +2 Trust with commons-aligned NPCs
- Rival accused — believe or doubt

---

## Reputation Impacts

- +2 **Respect** — fair cooperative outcome
- −2 **Trust** — proven hoarding or cheat
- +1 **Public Perception** — if Hall echoes good outcome (optional)

---

## Big Brother Beat (Subtle)

Kingdom hears exaggerated result via Hall resonance — "candidate starved nobles" vs "candidate fed servants first."

---

## DM Guidance

- Challenge generates *conversation*, not combat
- Failure = social cost, not death

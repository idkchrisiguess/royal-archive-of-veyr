---

## Summary

Final Table, [[Crown Chamber]], Hall opens, monarch chosen at table, epilogues. **Winner not predetermined.** Losers remain worthy.

---

- One [[Crown Evaluation Framework|Crown question]] (table words it)

## Scene-by-Scene

1. **Final Table** — [[The First Table Hall]]: bread tradition; each speaks one truth.
2. **Crown moments** — [[Crown Chamber]]: individual or voluntary pairs; ambient warmth/cold only.
3. **The question** — DM poses one question aligned to campaign themes (examples in framework — not canon list).
4. **Choosing completes** — bells; doors open; steward weeps; outside vignettes (narrate vigils, bookmakers, families).
5. **Monarch** — table decides via growth arcs + player choice; Crown "accepts" diegetically.
6. **Epilogues** — each PC + NPC pillar: win or lose with purpose (see [[00 NPC Campaign Compendium]] arcs).

# Character Creation — Choosing Candidate

Every PC is a **plausible monarch** — flaws included.

---

## Requirements

- **Level 3** entry (advance to 10 across campaign)
- One **leadership philosophy** (see table)
- Tie to Veyr (Heartlands, Marches, Coast, faith, trade, etc.)
- Reason the Crown might have chosen you (unknown to you)

---

## Leadership Philosophies (PC)

| Philosophy | Example angle | Distinct from NPC pillar |
|:--|:--|:--|
| **Change** | Reform institutions | Not Seraphine's competence alone |
| **Equality** | Fair law, access | Not Elira's order |
| **Justice** | Accountability | Not Garrick's duty alone |
| **Freedom** | Autonomy, limits on power | Not Brindle's prosperity |
| **Knowledge** | Evidence, education | New |
| **Perspective** | Outsider, minority voice | New |
| **Innovation** | New methods | Not Cogwort mercantile frame |
| **Compassion** | Care for suffering | Overlaps Tobin — differentiate by scale |

NPC pillars remain: Duty, Prosperity, Stability, Security, Competence, Community, Truth.

---

## Questionnaire (Session Zero)

1. What do your supporters *believe* about you vs what is true?
2. Whose trust do you need most inside the Hall?
3. What would you sacrifice for the crown? What wouldn't you?
4. What does **worthiness** mean to you — and who taught you that?
5. Name one person outside the Hall you cannot disappoint.
6. What rumor would destroy you if it spread?

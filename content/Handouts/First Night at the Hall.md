# First Night at Saint Vaelor's Hall

After the doors seal, tradition demands **The First Table**

- Thirteen seats, no head of table.
- Every monarch ate here before the Crown chose them.
- Whether the Crown cares is unknown. Everyone cares anyway.

**Etiquette (folk, not law):**
- Do not toast yourself first.
- Do not refuse bread.
- Nicknames earned tonight may stick.

You are not rulers yet. You are possibilities.

# Relationship Engine


---

## Alliance Tokens

Each pair tracks:

| Token | Meaning |
|:--|:--|
| **Trust (0–3)** | Will they believe you in crisis? |
| **Favor owed** | Yes/No + one sentence what |
| **Obligation** | Family, debt, saved life |
| **Public?** | Kingdom knows alliance or secret |

Record on [[Alliance Tracker]].

---

## Forming Alliances

- **Scene requirement:** Mutual benefit stated or implied.
- **Test:** One challenge completed with shared credit.
- **Fracture:** Broken promise → Trust −2; Obligation may invert to Rivalry.

---

## Rivalry

Not evil — competing philosophies or personal wound.

| Trigger | Effect |
|:--|:--|
| Public contradiction | +1 drama; Perception split |
| Stolen credit | Rivalry locked until conversation scene |
| Family feud | [[Political Complication Table]] |

Rivals can still cooperate (bitter coalitions).

---

## Friendship

Separate from alliance: emotional support, no political requirement.

- **Signal:** Shared meal without agenda ([[The Hearth Below]]).
- **NPC examples:** [[Tobin Reed]]–[[Sir Garrick Thornfield]]; [[Fenwick Vale]]–[[Jester Corvin]].

---

## Romance (Optional)

- Never required for win or worthiness.
- Use **Bond scenes** (Gardens, Quiet Room) — same mechanical weight as deep friendship.
- If romance causes deception, Trust and Crown reflection matter — not morality lecture.

---

## Session Prompts

- Who did you speak to privately?
- Whose reputation did you defend or damage?
- What favor do you owe?

# Reputation at a Glance

The kingdom cannot see inside — but stories escape. Your fellow candidates see everything.

**Five whispers about you:**
- **Trust** — Do you keep your word?
- **Influence** — Do others follow your lead?
- **Respect** — Do they believe you capable?
- **Public perception** — What do families and bookmakers say?
- **Standing** — Do peers treat you as worth following?

Ask the DM how your actions shifted the whispers. There is no published crown score — only who you are becoming.

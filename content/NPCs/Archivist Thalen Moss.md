# Archivist Thalen Moss

![[Archivist Thalen Moss - Portrait.png]]

## Appearance

Middle-aged human; spectacles on a chain, soft hands, ink under nails. Moves quietly as if libraries taught him to occupy no space.

Keeper of the Crown's public archives in the capital — not the Crown itself, but every Choosing record for three centuries.

### Clothing

Archivist's gray with silver thread — office uniform, not vanity.

## Character

### Personality

Patient. Precise. Asks for sources before opinions.

### Motives - Goals

A ruler who ignores history repeats it. Knowledge must be shared, not hoarded by houses.

### Values - Ideals

Knowledge

### Flaws

Can treat people as footnotes. Hides loneliness behind facts. Afraid of being wrong in public.

## Connections

### Work

- [[Lady Seraphine Ashcroft]] — respects methodology
- [[Jester Corvin]] — only person who makes him laugh at errors

### Other

- [[Aldric Penn]] — ally and argument
- [[Mother Elira Voss]] — scripture vs record

## History

Never sought throne. Summoned after publishing a corrected Choosing timeline that embarrassed three houses.

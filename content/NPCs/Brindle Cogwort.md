# Brindle Cogwort

![[Brindle Cogwort - Portrait.png]]


## Appearance

A compact goblin dressed like a successful entrepreneur trying to look twice as successful.

Bright waistcoats.  
Too many rings.  
Impossibly organized ledgers.

Brindle always seems to have acquired something interesting.

Many nobles underestimate him. Many merchants fear him. Brindle built his influence through trade networks, clever negotiations, and an uncanny ability to recognize opportunities before anyone else.

## Character

### Personality

Charming.  
Quick-witted.  
Restless.

He treats conversation as a game and every problem as a potential transaction.

Brindle genuinely enjoys bargaining.

### Motives - Goals

Prosperity creates stability.

Hungry people do not care about ideals.

A kingdom thrives when ordinary people can build better lives.

### Values - Ideals

Prosperity

### Flaws

He worries that people only value what he can provide.

His entire life has taught him that usefulness earns acceptance.

Without his success, who is he?

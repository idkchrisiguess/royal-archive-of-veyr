# Captain Mira Windale

![[Captain Mira Windale - Portrait.png]]

## Appearance

Weathered coastal captain; rope-calloused hands, short hair, laugh lines that disappear when she's calculating. Wears a pardon-letter seal on a chain — not jewelry, reminder.

Once commanded a privateer crew against pirates; pardoned for service. Chosen as Freedom embodied — the kingdom debates whether she is redemption or warning.

### Clothing

Sea-coat, boots, nothing that rusts.

## Character

### Personality

Bold. Direct. Protective of crew-minded "her people" even in the Hall.

### Motives - Goals

People deserve room to live without petty licenses and inherited permissions. Freedom with responsibility, not chaos.

### Values - Ideals

Freedom

### Flaws

Distrusts anyone who has never risked exile. Equates hesitation with cowardice — learning otherwise is her arc.

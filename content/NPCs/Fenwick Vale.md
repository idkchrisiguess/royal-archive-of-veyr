# Fenwick Vale

![[Fenwick Vale - Portrait.png]]


## Appearance

Weathered and practical.

Fenwick dresses for travel even when there is nowhere to travel to. Scars mark his arms and face, each one carrying a story he rarely tells.

He always seems prepared.

A renowned hunter of dangerous creatures and explorer of untamed places. People trust Fenwick in a crisis.

## Character

### Personality

Direct.  
Observant.  
Pragmatic.

He prefers action over theory.

Fenwick is rarely the loudest person in the room, but everyone notices when he speaks.

### Motives - Goals

A ruler's first responsibility is keeping people safe.

Nothing else matters if citizens live in fear.

Preparation is kindness.

### Values - Ideals

Security

### Flaws

He has spent so much of his life preparing for disasters that he struggles to imagine peace.

If danger vanished tomorrow, he would not know what to do with himself.

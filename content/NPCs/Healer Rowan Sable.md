# Healer Rowan Sable

>![[Healer Rowan Sable - Portrait.png]]


## Appearance

Human, early forties; gentle hands, tired eyes that brighten in crisis. Smell of herbs and iron. No house colors — only a silver circlet gift from a village he saved.

Field medic of the Western Marches; tended soldiers and refugees alike. Chosen as Compassion — some whisper the Crown wants a healer-king after the last monarch's long illness.

### Clothing

Healer's kit always within reach; fine clothes uncomfortable on him.

## Character

### Personality

Soft-spoken until someone is hurt; then commanding. Remembers names.

### Motives - Goals

Rule should feel like care at scale. Power is only worthy if it reduces suffering.

### Values - Ideals

Compassion

### Flaws

Cannot prioritize politically — wants to save everyone. Burned out twice; hides it.

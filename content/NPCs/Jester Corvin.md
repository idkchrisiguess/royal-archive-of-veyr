# Jester Corvin

![[Jester Corvin - Portrait.png]]


## Appearance

Colorful but intentionally difficult to read.

Corvin dresses like someone who remembers court fashion and then deliberately misapplies it. Nothing quite matches.

People often mistake him for ridiculous.

That is usually his intention.

Former court fool. Dismissed by many. Remembered by others as the man who could mock powerful people without losing his head.

## Character

### Personality

Funny.  
Perceptive.  
Unpredictable.

Corvin notices everything.

He often asks questions that sound absurd before revealing an uncomfortable insight.

### Motives - Goals

Truth is the foundation of trust.

People cannot solve problems they refuse to acknowledge.

A ruler should hear uncomfortable realities before flattering lies.

### Values - Ideals

Truth

### Flaws

Corvin has spent years exposing everyone else's masks.

He is no longer certain what remains of his own.

Humor protects him from vulnerability.

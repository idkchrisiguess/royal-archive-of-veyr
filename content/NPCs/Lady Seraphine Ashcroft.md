# Lady Seraphine Ashcroft
![[Lady Seraphine Ashcroft - Portrait.png]]


## Appearance

Elegant, poised, and meticulously presented.

Seraphine looks like nobility at its most refined. Her clothing is tasteful rather than extravagant, reflecting efficiency rather than vanity.

An exceptional administrator. Budgets balance when she oversees them. Projects finish on time. Cities under her supervision function remarkably well.

## Character

### Personality

Intelligent.  
Composed.  
Perfectionistic.

She notices details everyone else misses.

Seraphine often appears confident even when she is uncertain.

### Motives - Goals

Good intentions are meaningless without competence.

A ruler must be capable before they can be compassionate.

### Values - Ideals

Competence

### Flaws

She has spent her entire life becoming exceptional.

Now she wonders whether excellence is all she is.

Many people admire her achievements.

Very few know the person behind them.

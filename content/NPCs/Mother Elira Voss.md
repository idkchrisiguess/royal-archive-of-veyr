# Mother Elira Voss

![[Mother Elira Voss - Portrait.png]]


## Appearance

Elira projects calm before she speaks.

Her robes are simple but immaculate. Age has touched her features without diminishing her presence. She carries herself with the confidence of someone accustomed to giving guidance and being obeyed.

One of the kingdom's most respected spiritual leaders. People trust her judgment. Many politicians fear her influence.

## Character

### Personality

Measured.  
Intelligent.  
Patient.

Elira rarely reacts emotionally in public.

She asks questions more often than she gives answers.

### Motives - Goals

Stability allows people to flourish.

Freedom without structure eventually harms the vulnerable.

Institutions matter.

### Values - Ideals

Stability

### Flaws

She fears chaos more than she admits.

Her desire for order began as compassion but sometimes drifts toward control.

She genuinely wants to protect people.

She is not always certain where protection ends and authority begins.

# Sir Garrick Thornfield

![[Sir Garrick Thornfield - Portrait.png]]

## Appearance

Garrick looks exactly like the hero people expect him to be.

Tall, broad-shouldered, and perpetually composed, he wears himself with the posture of someone who has spent decades being watched. His armor is immaculate even when he is not preparing for battle. Every buckle is polished. Every movement is deliberate.

People notice him the moment he enters a room.

Garrick is the obvious candidate. He has defended villages from raiders, escorted diplomats through dangerous territory, and earned a reputation for unwavering integrity. Many people entering the Choosing would assume he is destined to win. He is the candidate most likely to receive immediate respect.

## Character

### Personality

Polite.  
Reliable.  
Patient.

Garrick rarely speaks first and rarely raises his voice. He listens carefully and answers thoughtfully.

He believes promises matter.

When others argue, Garrick often becomes the person everyone looks toward to settle disputes.

### Motives - Goals

A ruler exists to serve.

The crown is a burden, not a reward.

Good leaders do what is necessary even when it hurts.

### Values - Ideals

Duty

### Flaws

Garrick secretly fears that he has spent so long becoming what others needed that he no longer knows who he is without his obligations.

Every decision is filtered through duty.

He has become excellent at serving expectations.

He is no longer certain he possesses desires of his own.

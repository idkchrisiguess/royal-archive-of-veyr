# Tobin Reed

![[Tobin Reed - Portrait.png]]

## Appearance

Warm and approachable.

Tobin looks more like someone's favorite neighbor than a royal candidate. Flour stains seem to appear on his clothing no matter how carefully he dresses.

A village baker. Most people hearing his name wonder why he was chosen. Those who know him understand immediately.

## Character

### Personality

Kind.  
Patient.  
Genuine.

People tell Tobin things.

He never seems to ask for influence, yet influence follows him everywhere.

### Motives - Goals

A kingdom is a community before it is an institution.

People support leaders who know them.

Relationships matter more than titles.

### Values - Ideals

Community

### Flaws

Tobin constantly doubts he belongs among the other candidates.

He sees their accomplishments and assumes they must be more qualified.

He rarely recognizes how much people trust him.

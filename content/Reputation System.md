# Reputation System

Tracks how the Hall and kingdom *perceive* candidates. Affects alliances, information access, and **framework** for [[Crown Evaluation Framework]] — not a win meter.

---

## Five Tracks (0–5 each, or −2 to +3 for scandal)

| Track | Measures | Affects |
|:--|:--|:--|
| **Trust** | Reliability, kept promises | Alliances, private confidences |
| **Influence** | Ability to sway votes of conscience | Coalition challenges |
| **Respect** | Competence, dignity | NPC deference, challenge roles |
| **Public Perception** | Kingdom narrative (rumors, vigils) | Family pressure, bookmakers |
| **Candidate Standing** | Peers' willingness to follow | Leadership trials |

Use [[Reputation Tracker]] at table.

---

## Changing Reputation

| Action | Typical shift |
|:--|:--|
| Keep public promise | +1 Trust |
| Break confidence (proven) | −2 Trust, −1 Standing |
| Win challenge with grace | +1 Respect |
| Humiliate rival | +1 Influence short-term, −1 Perception |
| Serve kitchen anonymously | +1 Trust (if discovered), +1 Perception |
| Visible panic | −1 Respect (recoverable) |

**Hall nudge:** Reputation shifts may echo in [[Whisper Gallery]] or Commons board — not announced scores.

---

## Tiers (Narrative)

| Total (approx.) | Kingdom whisper |
|:--|:--|
| 15+ | "Future monarch" |
| 10–14 | "Serious contender" |
| 5–9 | "Dark horse" or "Polarizing" |
| 0–4 | "Survivor" or "Scandal" |

---

## Crown (Framework)

Crown does not sum points. Use tracks to **prompt reflection scenes** when a candidate peaks in Influence but crashes in Trust.

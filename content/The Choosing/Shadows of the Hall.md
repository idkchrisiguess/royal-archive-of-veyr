# The Departure Ceremony

After the vote:

The candidate is brought before the Crown.

For the first time in history, the table sees what actually happens.

The candidate places their hand upon the Crown.

The Hall falls silent.

The Crown speaks.

Not publicly.

Only to them.

The candidate leaves through a door that has never previously opened.

Everyone assumes they are gone.

Instead they enter:

# The Council of the Fallen

A hidden wing of Saint Vaelor's Hall.

A place known only through rumor.

Candidates have always known eliminated rulers go somewhere.

Nobody has ever known where.

---

# What the Fallen Learn

Each elimination grants:

## One Revelation

The player receives a secret.

Examples:

- Truth about a previous monarch
- History of the Hall
- Information about another candidate
- Secret passages
- Hidden challenge mechanics
- Fragments of the Crown's purpose

Never enough to solve the mystery.

Just enough to create leverage.

---

# The Council Chamber

The Fallen are not prisoners.

They possess:

### Private Meeting Rooms

Finalists may request audiences.

### Observation Galleries

Can watch portions of the Hall unseen.

### Archives

Contain records unavailable to active candidates.

### Hidden Corridors

Allow movement through forgotten parts of the Hall.

---

# Influence Currency

Give the Fallen a resource.

Call it:

## Favor of the Crown

or

## Echoes

or

## Remnants

Each eliminated candidate gains one point immediately.

Additional points are earned by:

- solving mysteries
- uncovering secrets
- assisting Hall officials
- accomplishing side objectives

They spend these points for influence.

---

# Council Powers

## Minor Powers

Cost 1

Examples:

- Deliver anonymous message
- Reveal clue
- Grant small challenge bonus
- Learn secret vote

---

## Moderate Powers

Cost 2

Examples:

- Force private meeting
- Give challenge advantage
- Protect candidate from nomination tie-break
- Reveal partial challenge information

---

## Major Powers

Cost 3+

Examples:

- Nullify a vote
- Force public confession
- Open sealed archives
- Compel audience with a Hall official

---

# The Hall's Secret Economy

This creates an incredible dynamic.

Imagine:

Sir Garrick votes out Mother Elira.

Three rounds later:

Mother Elira possesses knowledge nobody else has.

Now Garrick desperately needs her support.

The relationship changes.


# Final Five Twist

When only five candidates remain:

The Council becomes public.

The Hall opens the chamber.

For the first time:

The active candidates see where the eliminated have been.

See what they've learned.

See what they've become.

The people they discarded now hold enormous influence.

---

# Endgame Role

The Fallen should not choose the monarch.

That belongs to the Crown.

However they can influence:

- Final challenges
- Information available
- Candidate reputations
- Opportunities presented

They become kingmakers without becoming kings.

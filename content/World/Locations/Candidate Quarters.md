# Candidate Quarters

**Master:** [[Saint Vaelor's Hall]] · **Index:** [[Hall Locations Compendium]]

## Description

Thirteen suites (also called [[Candidate Galleries]]); balconies over gardens.

Sleep, letters, private crisis.

Neighbors swap after betrayal or reconciliation.

## NPC Uses

[[Lady Seraphine Ashcroft]] schedules; [[Brindle Cogwort]] noise treaties; new candidates claim corners.

## Secrets

Balcony whispers carry to [[Whisper Gallery]] on windy nights.

## Events

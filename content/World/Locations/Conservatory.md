# Conservatory

**Master:** [[Saint Vaelor's Hall]] · **Index:** [[Hall Locations Compendium]]

## Description

Glass roof, citrus and river reeds, harp that no one admits playing.

## Purpose

Rest, music nights, private conversation with kingdom muffled.

## Hall Behaviors

Plants thrive near honest conversation; wilt near sustained pretense (subtle).

## NPC Uses

[[Healer Rowan Sable]] tends herbs; [[Captain Mira Windale]] hates feeling trapped in glass.

## Secrets

Hidden bench behind orchids — acoustics contain secrets.

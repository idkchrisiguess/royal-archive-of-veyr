# Grand Atrium

**Master:** [[Saint Vaelor's Hall]] · **Index:** [[Hall Locations Compendium]]

## Description

Vaulted stone and amber glass; thirteen pillars with proverbs on service. Sunlight pools; smells of wood, beeswax, rain.

## Purpose

Threshold Ceremony; rare kingdom-wide resonance moments.

## Hall Behaviors

- Whispers carry to wrong pillars.
- Frost on glass after emotional scenes (hands, chains — interpretive).

## NPC Uses

[[Sir Garrick Thornfield]] center of gravity; [[Jester Corvin]] refuses performative honesty here.

## Secrets

Thirteenth pillar warms at Threshold touch — tradition only.

## Events

Session 01; Act III assembly.

## Story Hooks

Overlapping oath echoes — repeat or expose?

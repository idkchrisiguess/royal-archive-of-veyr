## History 

### The Fractured Century

Succession crises, noble wars, slow weakening — invasion was not the threat; *Veyr was*.

### Founding of the Choosing

Last heir of the Fractured Century could not unite claims. The [[The Crown]] appeared (origin disputed: archmages, philosophers, saints, monarchs). Selection repeated; opposition faded → tradition → law → identity.

### Saint Vaelor

Figure of unity in culture; identity disputed (king, philosopher, myth). Agreement: **leadership is service.** [[Saint Vaelor's Hall]] is symbolic heart of the Choosing.

## Society & Politics

- Nobility competes on **reputation** and producing candidates, not guaranteed inheritance.
- Religion: plural, cooperative; influence through moral authority ([[Mother Elira Voss]]).
- National values: responsibility, reputation, service, practical wisdom, community.
- Suspicion of extremes; proverb: *"A ruler is not measured by promises, but by people."*
